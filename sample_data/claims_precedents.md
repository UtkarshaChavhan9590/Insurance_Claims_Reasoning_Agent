# Claims Precedent Log (Sample Historical Decisions)

## Precedent 001 — Minor Collision, Parking Lot
Claim amount: $850 (collision, bumper damage). Documentation complete (photos,
repair estimate, driver statement). No police report (not required, under $1,000).
No fraud indicators. **Decision: Auto-approved.** Rationale: under $2,000 threshold,
complete documentation, no red flags.

## Precedent 002 — Windshield Crack, Hailstorm
Claim amount: $300 (comprehensive, weather damage). Documentation: photos and
repair estimate provided. **Decision: Auto-approved.** Rationale: comprehensive
claims under deductible threshold with documentation are low-risk.

## Precedent 003 — Claim Filed 5 Days After Policy Start
Claim amount: $4,500 (collision). Documentation complete. Fraud indicator triggered:
claim filed within 14 days of policy inception. **Decision: Escalated to fraud
analyst**, regardless of documentation completeness, per Section 5 policy.

## Precedent 004 — Rideshare Driver, No Endorsement
Claim amount: $1,200 (collision while driver app was active). Policyholder did not
have Rideshare Endorsement (Form RS-100). **Decision: Denied** — excluded under
Section 3, with explanation provided to claimant and guidance on adding the
endorsement for future coverage.

## Precedent 005 — Large Claim, Clean History
Claim amount: $6,000 (collision, multi-vehicle accident). Documentation complete,
police report attached. No fraud indicators. **Decision: Routed to human adjuster**
per Section 6 ($2,000+ threshold) — adjuster approved after review. Note: AI agent's
recommendation (approve) matched the human adjuster's final decision, demonstrating
